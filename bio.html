BIO

Lookfar Camera was started by Jon Brundage Jr., a Brooklyn-based Director of Photography and Camera Operator. Jon has worked in television production for over twelve years and has shot on numerous shows on networks such as HBO, AMC, Nat Geo, Bravo, VH1, Food Network, Comedy Central, HGTV and others. Jon has travelled the globe making content, having shot on five continents and in most states across the U.S.

In his spare time, Jon can be found playing hockey, trying to keep up with his wife on a run, or finishing the crossword. Based in wonderful Crown Heights, Jon thoroughly enjoys all the adventures that Brooklyn has to offer.
